## Open to Everyone

Our Thursday evening sitting is the heart of our community week. It is completely free, requires no booking, and welcomes everyone — whether you have been meditating for decades or are curious for the first time.

## Format

- **6:00pm** — Doors open, quiet settling time
- **6:15pm** — Guided meditation (30 minutes)
- **6:45pm** — Short teaching or reading (15 minutes)
- **7:00pm** — Informal tea and conversation

## What to Expect

We keep things simple and unhurried. There is no expectation to speak, participate, or share. Come as you are.

A donation bowl is by the door. All donations support our scholarship fund, which helps those who cannot afford our courses attend free of charge.
