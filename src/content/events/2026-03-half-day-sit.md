## A Day of Deepening

Half-day sessions offer something different from a weekly class: the chance to settle in, let the busyness of the week dissolve, and spend a sustained period in practice.

We will work with breath meditation and open awareness, moving through the arc of a full sitting day in miniature.

Suitable for those who have some experience of meditation, either from our courses or elsewhere.

## Format

- 9:00am — Arrival and settling
- 9:15am — Guided meditation
- 10:30am — Tea break
- 10:45am — Silent sitting
- 11:30am — Teaching and discussion
- 1:00pm — Close

A simple lunch is available afterwards for those who wish to stay and connect.
