## About the Retreat

The spring season invites renewal. This three-day residential retreat creates a container of silence, practice, and gentle inquiry — a rare opportunity to step back from the pace of daily life and reconnect with what matters most.

Led by Sarah Morrison and David Chen, the retreat draws on shamatha (calm abiding), the practice of rest, and teachings on natural awareness from both the Tibetan and Zen traditions.

## Schedule Overview

Each day follows a rhythm of sitting practice, gentle movement, teaching, and free time in nature. Meals are taken in silence. Evenings include optional group discussion.

- Saturday arrival from 4pm
- Practice begins Saturday evening
- Sunday full day of practice
- Monday practice and closing by 3pm

## What is Included

Room and board (shared accommodation), all meals (vegetarian), teaching, and practice sessions.

Bring comfortable clothing, a journal, and an open heart.
